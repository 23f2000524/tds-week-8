# Customer Analytics: Purchase Amount Distribution by Customer Segment

White Johnston specializes in advanced analytics to help organizations understand and optimize customer relationships.  
This visualization was created for a major retail client to analyze how customer spending varies across defined customer segments.

The analysis is intended for use in executive presentations, quarterly business reviews, and strategic planning.  
A Seaborn boxplot was selected for its statistical rigor and professional visual design.

---

## üìä Visualization Output

The included chart (`chart.png`) shows the distribution of purchase amounts across the following synthetic customer segments:

- **Budget**
- **Mid-Tier**
- **Premium**
- **VIP**

These values simulate realistic customer purchase patterns, enabling visual insight into spending behavior within each group.

The visualization highlights:

- Median purchase value differences across segments  
- Outlier presence (common among Premium and VIP spenders)  
- Clear segmentation of customer value tiers
